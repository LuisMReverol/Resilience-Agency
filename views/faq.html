What is BAFA?

BAFA is the authority responsible for export control, economic development for SME’s, etc. Our consulting services are based on its framework.

Learn more

What is the BAFA subsidy for?

The German government can fully subsidise in advance up to 4000€ to be used by SMEs for business consulting services through a certified consultancy like ours.

Learn more

What is the KfW Quick Loan? Am I eligible?

Enterprises with 10 or more employees that were active on the market before January 1, 2019 may be eligible to receive a rapid loan of up to 800,000€.

Learn more

What is resilience? How can your agency help me build it?

Resilience is the ability to cope with hardships. We combine expertise and innovation to help you adapt quickly and rise above challenges.

Learn more

What can your consultants advise me on?

We can offer innovative and pragmatic solutions related to going digital, saving money, expanding products and services, managing personnel, and much more.

What are your consulting qualifications?

Not only are we certified by the German government to provide consulting based on the BAFA framework, but we also have many diverse business experiences to draw from.
